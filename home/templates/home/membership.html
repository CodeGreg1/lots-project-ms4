How to Join

The LONDON OMNIBUS TRACTION SOCIETY brings together those interested in London’s buses, routes and ancillary transport services. We study, record and report on bus operators and operations in and around London, to a radius of about 36 miles from Charing Cross.

Members receive our monthly journal THE LONDON BUS, which records route alterations, tendering updates, vehicle allocations, together with a selection of topical photographs.

We also publish the quarterly LONDON BUS MAGAZINE. This features modern and historical articles, reviews of London bus events, and recent news in pictures. We regard this as our flagship publication

We hold meetings in central London, where members can buy our publications at a discount from our sales stand just as they can at the various rallies we attend or post-free from our Postal Sales department.

We look forward to hearing from you. Clicking any of the buttons below will take you to the payment stage where you can pay online or fill in a form and post it to us.
