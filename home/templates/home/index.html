How to join!

Welcome to LOTS


The London Omnibus Traction Society was founded in 1964, and now has one of the largest memberships of any bus enthusiasts' organisation in the UK.

For those who are already members, Welcome to our new website! And to those London bus enthusiasts who have yet to join we are proud to offer you the many benefits of doing so. So please enjoy our website and see what’s out there!


Membership


Becoming a LOTS member means you’ll receive both our information-packed TLB Newsletter and our quarterly London Bus Magazine (or whichever one of these you choose).

Not only will you receive annual subscriptions for our monthly and quarterly publications, but you will get exclusive access to our monthly meetings too.

By joining you will also become part of a friendly and welcoming community of London bus enthusiasts and benefit from a wealth of knowledge and expertise. So join us on a fantastic bus journey from Aylesbury to Sevenoaks and Harlow to Virginia Water with the whole of London in between!
